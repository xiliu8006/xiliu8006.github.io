Research

I'm interested in computer vision/graphics, generative AI, and automomous driving. Most of my research is about inferring editable video and 3D scene generation, and 3D world understanding.

Bézier Splatting for Fast and Differentiable Vector Graphics
Xi Liu*, Chaoyi Zhou*, Nanxuan Zhao, Siyu Huang,
NeurIPS, 2025
project page / arXiv

This work introduces a new differentiable VG representation, dubbed Bézier splatting, that enables fast yet high-fidelity VG rasterization.

3DGS-Enhancer: Enhancing Unbounded 3D Gaussian Splatting with View-consistent 2D Diffusion Priors
Xi Liu*, Chaoyi Zhou*, Siyu Huang,
NeurIPS, 2024
project page / arXiv

We leverage 2D video diffusion priors to address the challenging 3D view consistency problem, reformulating it as achieving temporal consistency within a video generation process.

Latent Radiance Fields with 3D-aware 2D Representations
Chaoyi Zhou*, Xi Liu*, Feng Luo, Siyu Huang
ICLR, 2025  
project page / arXiv / code

In this work, we propose a method to achieve 3D-aware 2D representations and enable 3D reconstruction in the latent space.
